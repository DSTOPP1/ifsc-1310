Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Segway Centaur?

The Segway Centaur is a concept vehicle designed to provide innovative and exciting personal transportation for young professionals and tech enthusiasts.

When will the Centaur be available?

The Segway Centaur is currently a concept vehicle. Stay tuned for announcements on availability!

How does the Centaur differ from other Segway products?

Unlike traditional Segway products, the Centaur is designed to combine agility, speed, and versatility in a stylish package.
